Cold Brew 42

Welcome!

Welcome to Cold Brew 42, an energetic and passionate band that creates music to inspire and connect with our audience. Our music is a fusion of various genres, including rock, alternative, and pop. We started as a group of three talented musicians who came together to create something unique and special.

Chuck Cullen, our lead guitarist, brings his extensive experience in the music industry, while Mike Farley, our vocalist, has a powerful and soulful voice that can captivate any audience. Dan Transue, our bassist, is a self-taught musician who adds a unique flavor to our sound.

Together, Cold Brew 42 creates music that is inspired by our individual influences and experiences. Our goal is to create music that touches the hearts and souls of our listeners. Join us on this musical journey as we continue to create and share our passion with the world.
